THERE ARE MANY REASONS TO COME TO ALIMENTEC BOGOTÁ 2020 – HERE ARE SOME OF THE BEST:

1. PROFIT FROM A NO. 1 BASE!

Alimentec is the leading trade fair for the food & drink as well as the hospitality industry in Colombia and the Andean region. The most comprehensive buyer groups will be present, from Colombia as well as from Latin America and the Caribbean. It is the leading platform for qualified business contacts and the perfect base for your success in the region! Colombia is the perfect hub to expand your business in the fast-growing markets in the Andean region and Central America.

2. PROFIT FROM POWERFUL PARTNERS!

Alimentec is “powered by Anuga”, the world’s leading trade fair in food & beverage. Corferias and Koelnmesse are the organisers – which means you will benefit from the combined power of two strong players, their excellent know-how and their widespread networks.

3. PROFIT FROM A PROMISING MARKET!

Colombia is the 3rd largest economy in Latin America! 48.5 million inhabitants, a growing middle class and a young population make it a really promising market: Almost 1/3 of private household consumption in Colombia goes into the food & beverage sector and rising disposable incomes boost the demand further.

4. PROFIT FROM A GROWING HORECA SECTOR!

Tourism is also growing fast, surpassing even coffee, flowers and bananas as a foreign income generator! Together with higher spending in restaurants and cafes, the HORECA sector benefits heavily from the country’s positive development.

10 TRADE SHOWS IN ONE.

1. FINE FOOD:

The largest of the Anuga trade fairs is dedicated to delicatessen, gourmet and basic foods – the meeting point for manufacturers and importers from all over the world.

2. FROZEN FOOD:

Expierence the developments of the frozen food market first-hand. Here you can meet up with both - the retail trade and the out-ofhome market.

3. MEAT:

At the largest meat trade fair in the world the themes meat, poultry and sausages are presented in different halls in a tangible way and the trends of the industry are depicted.

4. CHILLED & FRESH FOOD:

The food trends "togo", "ready-to-eat", fresh delicatessen and snacks are as relevant as ever and are thus once again present in a trade show for everything "fresh out of the refrigerator".

5. DAIRY:

The world's largest leading international trade fair for the milk and dairy industry provides detailed information about new products, innovative production methods and eating trends.

6. BREAD & BAKERY:

The highest international level trade fair is the meeting point for everyone who is involved in the very complex market for bread and bakery products - for the retail trade or bulk consumers.

7. DRINKS:

Anuga Drinks is the largest trading place in the world for non-alcoholic beverages, alcoholic specialities and flavourings. Fruit juices are particularly under focus at Anuga.

8. ORGANIC:

The large organic offer from all over the globe particularly underlines the international character of Anuga. Under focus: Trend themes such as protected origin, vegan diets and fair trade.

9. HOT BEVERAGES:

Coffee and tea are rapidly developing into their own market. This development is being reflected at Anuga in the scope of a special trade show for all hot beverage experts.

10. CULINARY CONCEPT:

Discover a symbiosis between cooking presentations and contests, an exhibition and infos on the latest food service trends how the tastes of the world inspire professional chefs.
